Our Contribution

Over the past 6 months, Team ACE has tackled the problem of the high energy consumption in school by raising awareness of the carelessness of students and staff alike by holding presentations for Year 3-6. Similarly, we've suggested different energy saving methods to heads of schools to lower energy consumption and raise awareness in meetings with the heads of the school to plan and encourage energy saving.

What can you do?

Start small, switching off the lights, aircon and projectors as you leave the classroom can really make a difference. To take it one step further, use more energy saving solutions to basic problems. For example, turn the aircon up a couple degrees and add a fan instead of having the aircon on the lowest setting.
