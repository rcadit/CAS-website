SUSTAINABILITY AT TANGLIN TRUST SCHOOL

At Tanglin Trust School, we strive to create a sustainible future through our everyday actions.

Respect

From a young age, our students are encouraged to respect the environment - this attidude is what motivates us to restore the environment we live in.


Responsibility

Here at Tanglin, we help our students develop a sense of responsibility for the environment. Recently we introduced many new recycling bins around the school so the waste that we produce is recycled responsibly.

At Tanglin we believe that, with the right efforts, we can do our part and achieve our goal of saving the environment.
