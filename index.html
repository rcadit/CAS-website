Sustainibility at Tanglin Trust School

At Tanglin Trust School, we strive to create a sustainible future through our everyday actions.
Find out how you can make a difference
Respect
From a young age, our students are encouraged to respect the environment - this attitude is what motivates us to restore the environment we live in.
Responsibility
Here at Tanglin, we help our students develop a sense of responsibility for the environment. Recently, we introduced many new recycling bins around the school so the waste that we produce is recycled responsibly.
Purpose
At Tanglin we believe that, with the right efforts, we can do our part and achieve our goal of saving the environment. We have recently introduced En-Trak, a platform that helps us understand and monitor our energy usage and its affects on the environment
