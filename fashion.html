Threads

Who we are

"Threads" is a group of students interested in the impact of the fast fashion industry on our environment. We focus on trying to reduce clothing waste by offering alternatives to throwing away clothes that no longer fit, aren't trendy anymore, or just not to your taste: upcycling!
Upcycling, similar to recycling, takes something that's about to be thrown away and transforms it into something new.

Our Contribution

Threads has been collecting unwanted clothes from the student body to upcycle into new designs, the products of which will be showcased at our fashion show in eco-week.

What can you do?

To support Threads, you can attend our fashion show to find out more about the fast fashion industry, upcycling, and what more you can do to reduce clothing waste
